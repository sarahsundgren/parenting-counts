Pre-Program Self Evaluation
It is our goal to ensure that the Parenting Counts Professional Program helps professionals increase their knowledge, confidence and change positive parenting behaviors in a variety of communities. To achieve this goal, we have created a Pre-Test and Post-Test to accompany the Parenting Counts Online Training. Please complete the following questions. You will not receive a score as this is not a test of your learning. Your answers will be anonymous and will be used for data collection purposes only. This Evaluation is not part of the Continuing Education Assessments that are required at the end of each Pillar.
